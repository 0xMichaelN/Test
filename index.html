About

A team of passionate community builders are bringing 8,000 MetaWomen NFTs to the Ethereum blockchain.

The MetaWomenNFT2 collection will represent all women from different walks of life: artists, photographers, marketers, coders, business women, and free-thinkers. These women represent different nationalities, personalities, and sexualities. Every woman and man is welcome and invited to join the MetaWomenNFT2 project and support our mission.

The project's mission is to build on the skills women already possess and help them take advantage of new digital opportunities that are becoming available in the market of Web-3 and the development of the Metaverse. Web-2 skills such as sales, clerical, managerial, web development often times just need upskilling to enable a candidate to become qualified for a Web-3 career.

The MetaWomenNFT2 team believes that with the right support and training, everyday women and men can change the direction of their career path into the digital world. The team will use the virtual MetaClub to provide both of the needed resources free of charge to the members.

FAQ

What is MetaWomenNFT2?
MetaWomenNFT2 is a female-led NFT collection comprised of 8,000 MetaWomen NFTs.

The MetaWomenNFT2 collection is part of MetaClubLLC, and it is the first phase of a long term vision for the project.
How many NFTs are in the MetaWomenNFT2 collection?
The MetaWomenNFT2 collection contains 8,000 NFTs.
When does the MetaWomenNFT2 sale begin?
The presale begins on Friday, April 1st and ends on Sunday, April 3rd. The public sale begins on Monday, April 4th.
What is the mint price?
The pre-sale mint price is 0.06 ethereum, and the public sale price is 0.08 ethereum.
How do holders claim their free online course that comes with a MetaWomenNFT2 purchase?
When the MetaWomenNFT2 collection is sold out, MetaWomenNFT2 holders will be able to authenticate thier wallet in the MetaClub and select an online course to enroll in for free.

Each NFT in the MetaWomenNFT2 collection can be used to redeem an online course in the MetaClub one time.
Will the MetaClub be available to those that do not own a MetaWomenNFT2?
The MetaClub will be available on a limited basis by invite only.
